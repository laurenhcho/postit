Post it! — Interactive Demo

Upload a flyer or create an event. Browse, save, and sync to your calendar.

How to use

Discover
Swipe right or tap ✓ to save an event. Swipe left or tap ✕ to skip. The calendar button saves and adds to Google Calendar. Filter events using the category pills at the top.
Saved
All events you swiped right on. Tap Remove to unsave. Events are synced to your Google Calendar automatically.
Buckets
Shared event playlists with friends. Tap + Invite Friends to add people by name. Create a new bucket with + New Bucket.
Map
See all campus events pinned on a live CMU map. Tap any event row to see its details.
Profile
Follow organizations to get notified when they post. Tap Settings to manage calendar sync and reminder preferences.
+ Create
Upload a flyer image — details are extracted automatically — or fill the form manually. Start typing a building name and CMU locations will autofill.
