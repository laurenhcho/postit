How we worked

Our collaboration process

This team values each other's time and expects high-quality collaboration from this project. We want to learn from each other's strengths and make up for our individual weaknesses and points of conflict peacefully. We agree to respect each other by being communicative about our expectations and what we can responsibly manage.

We divided the project into four workstreams — research, design, strategy, and feasibility — with each team member owning a lane while contributing to a shared vision. The prototype was built iteratively from paper sketches to the final working demo.

💬Main channel: iMessage group chat. Respond within 24 hours.
📁All documents shared in Google Drive. Full version history kept.
🖥️Meetings via Zoom or asynchronously based on availability.
✅If you might miss a deadline, message the team early — not after.
